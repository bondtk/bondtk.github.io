Community App

Buckwood Connect

A complete community app for a private campground, built from the ground up.

500+ active users across iOS and Android — running flawlessly in production.
App Store Google Play

The Story

From paper sign-up sheets to a full community platform.

Buckwood Connect started as a volunteer project: bring a private campground community fully online, in one app, instead of scattered across paper sign-up sheets, group texts, and word of mouth. It's now a full-featured platform that the entire campground community relies on every season — handling real check-ins, real payments, and real conversations.

Features

Everything a community needs in one place.

Check-In & Member Management

Streamlined check-in process for campers and seasonal residents.

Grill Ordering

Live food ordering with Square-integrated payments and direct-to-kitchen thermal printing.

Community Wall

A shared space for announcements, photos, and conversation.

Private Messaging

Direct chat between members, with emoji tapbacks.

Emergency Alerts

Critical, attention-grabbing notifications that reach every member instantly when it matters most.

Q&A

A place for members to ask questions and get answers from the community.

User Profiles

Personal bio pages so members can connect.

Rentals & Events

Manage and browse what's available and what's happening on-site.

Geofencing & Auto Check-Out

Smart location awareness that keeps check-in status accurate without manual upkeep.

Built For Real Use

Not a concept. A platform people depend on.

This isn't a concept app — it's in daily use, processing live transactions, sending real alerts, and supporting an active community across an entire camping season. When issues come up, they get resolved fast, because real people are depending on it working.
