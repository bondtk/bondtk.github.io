Apple TV Party Game

Sketchy Friends!

A drawing party game for Apple TV — built for laughs with the people in the room.

App Store

How It Works

Your TV is the host. Your phone is the controller.

Sketchy Friends! turns your Apple TV into the host of the party. Everyone joins from their own phone — just scan a QR code on screen, no extra hardware or app downloads required for guessing along.

Each round, players draw a secret prompt on their phone while everyone else watches it come to life on the big screen. Then the guessing begins: the better your friends can guess what you drew, the more points you earn — and the better your drawing, the more points everyone gets for guessing right. It rewards both creativity and cleverness, and the results are usually hilarious.

Bilingual by Design

Language is never the reason someone sits out.

Every prompt and word appears in both English and Spanish, so language is never the reason someone sits out the fun. Whether your group is bilingual, all-Spanish-speaking, or just mixed company, everyone plays on equal footing.

Why I Built It

I wanted a party game that felt as good to play as it looks — simple enough that anyone picks it up in seconds, built to bring people together regardless of what language they speak, and designed specifically to take advantage of Apple TV as a shared, social screen rather than a solo-play device.
