QuickSell Pro

Turn your garage sale, estate sale, or resale event into a real point-of-sale system.

App Store

The Story

From price tags to a real point of sale.

QuickSell Pro started as a way to fix the chaos of garage sale day — handwritten price tags, lost track of what sold, a shoebox full of cash and no real record of any of it. What began as a simple inventory and barcode tool has grown into a genuinely powerful point-of-sale system, one that's simple enough to set up in minutes but capable enough to run a full sale from start to finish.

What It Does

Everything a real sale needs.

Inventory, the easy way

Snap a photo, add a description and price, and you're done. Items can be bulk-imported from a spreadsheet, tracked by quantity, and managed with quick swipe-to-edit and swipe-to-delete gestures.

Real checkout, not a guess

A floating cart shows your running total as you go. Apply sales tax and cart-wide discounts in a tap, calculate cash change automatically, or accept digital payments on the spot — just save your Zelle, PayPal, and Cash App QR codes right in the app, and pull up whichever one your customer prefers at checkout.

Scan it like a real store

Print professional barcode labels — sized for standard Avery sheets — and ring items up with your phone's camera or a Bluetooth scanner. Smart scan logic keeps the line moving and prevents accidental duplicate scans.

Know exactly where you stand

A visual sales dashboard shows revenue trends, your top sellers, and a cash-vs-digital breakdown at a glance. Refund or void a sale in a tap — inventory and totals update automatically, no manual math required.

Multi-family sale? No problem

Owner settlements automatically track exactly what's owed to each person — Mom, Dad, Aunt Mary — by the end of the day, no separate spreadsheet needed.

Everything stays in sync

Price items on your iPhone, then run the sale from your iPad — inventory, sales history, and settings sync automatically across your devices through iCloud, no subscription required.

Why I Built It

A business tool, not a hobby.

This was a project I'd wanted to build for years — and watching it grow from a simple labeling tool into something that genuinely replaces a cash register and a notebook has been one of the most satisfying parts of doing this work. If you're clearing out a garage or running a full estate sale, QuickSell Pro is built to handle it like a business, not a hobby.
