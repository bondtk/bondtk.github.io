Services

Custom app development,
start to finish.

I build full custom mobile apps — from a raw idea to a live App Store and Google Play listing. Every project gets a native experience on both iOS and Android, built around how real people will actually use it, not just what's technically convenient.

I specialize in utility, productivity, and light entertainment apps — the kind of software that solves a real problem or makes something more enjoyable, without requiring an AAA-studio art and animation pipeline. If your idea fits that mold, I'm a great match.

How It Works

From idea to App Store, in five steps.

1

Discovery

We talk through your idea, your users, and what success looks like. No jargon required — just bring the problem you're trying to solve.

2

Design

I map out the experience: how it should feel to use, not just what it should do. This is where the "fun and easy" part comes from.

3

Build

Your app gets built using modern, AI-accelerated development — which means faster turnaround without cutting corners on quality.

4

Test

Every feature gets tested across devices and real-world scenarios before anything goes live. Bugs get caught here, not after launch.

5

Launch

I handle the App Store and Google Play submission process, so your app goes live the right way the first time.

After Launch

Ongoing support & maintenance.

Launch isn't the finish line. I offer ongoing support and maintenance plans — bug fixes, feature updates, OS compatibility updates, and the kind of fast response real users expect when something breaks.

500+ active users I currently support across iOS and Android for Buckwood Connect — including resolving and communicating through real production issues.
