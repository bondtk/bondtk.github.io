The Story

Raffle apps haven't kept up. This one does.

RaffleHQ was built to fix a real problem: most raffle apps look like they haven't been touched in over a decade — clunky, ad-riddled, and they charge you before you've even tried them. RaffleHQ does the opposite. It's free to start, runs beautifully on a modern iPhone, and is built to make whatever event you're running — a school fundraiser, a charity gala, a church raffle, a backyard party — actually feel like an event.

Three Ways to Draw

Pick the format that fits your event.

Free Drawing

The classic, done right. Add your participants, hit draw, and reveal your winner with a dramatic live announcement. Perfect for door prizes, giveaways, and team picks — free for everyone, no limits on how many you run.

Paid Raffle

A real ticketed raffle, handled with confidence. Set your ticket price, track exactly who paid, and let the built-in weighted draw engine account for multi-ticket holders automatically — every ticket gets its fair shot, no math required.

Reverse Raffle

The format your guests will be talking about afterward. Instead of picking one winner, RaffleHQ eliminates participants round by round, building suspense until only one person remains. Customize how many are eliminated each round and set podium placements — Grand Prize, 2nd, 3rd — for a finish that actually feels like one.

A Shared Experience

Not just a drawing — an event.

Guests join instantly with a simple 6-character code — no app download required to watch. They see winners revealed in real time on their own device, follow live eliminations, and watch the full reverse raffle podium ceremony unfold. Hosts can let guests self-register from their phone, or lock entry down to host-only for paid ticketed events — full control, either way.

Free to Start

Pro when you need it.

RaffleHQ is free to download with no account required and no credit card to get started. The free tier covers your first event — one active event, up to 25 participants, Free Drawing format, and the classic Sleek Dark theme.

RaffleHQ Pro unlocks unlimited participants and events, both Paid and Reverse Raffle formats, and every premium theme — with annual, monthly, or single-event pricing options. Full pricing details available on the App Store.
